Health Gaurantee

For all dogs you purchase from us we provide the following garuantees:

  • Heart, Liver, Lung, Kidney, and Bowel for life against any birth defect.
  • Hips-Full 2 year garuantee against dysplasia.
  • We garuantee eyes on any pup and young adult, we are not responsible as dog gets older.

All puppies are born at our home kennel, loving raised and very people/kid friendly. If one of the above birth defects should arise with your puppy, we kindly ask that you send us your vet records/diagnosis & we will give you another puppy. There will be NO refunds. We understand it is impossible to replace the puppy you loved. Our purpose is to provide great loving people with quality, kind Airedale pets. This agreement is only valid with the original owner/buyer however, this agreement is void if the owner/buyer sign or give over ownership of the dog. We will not pay any puppy/dog or veterinarian expenses incurred. For all dogs at our kennel we provide the utmost care and ensure that they are all taken care of.


The Story of the Blacks

Southern realizes when she is a little girl that she will dedicate her life to breeding dogs. After all, it is in her blood; she grew up with dogs in her father's kennel in the swamps of Alabama. A business woman who with her husband has devoted over 30 years of her life to breeding Airedale Terriers, specializing over time on a unique breed --- a Black Airedale. The growing acclaim of their Black Airedales is interrupted as they face an intimidating fight against intimidation and slander from competing breeders and the greed and corruption of lawyers and large socio-educational clubs. They are forced to defend their reputation and her career and their beloved dogs in the competitive world of dog breeding.
